0.7.2.0

[FIXED] Error while logged out. (DiFi: folder request failed)

[ADDED] Show only new item count. (set as default, old mode avaialable)

This is a big, important change.

Previously, if you had 110 messages, of which 10 were new, it would show 110 on green background. After you've seen the new messages, it would show 110 on red.

Now, it will show 10 on green background, and after you've seen the new messages, it will show no badge at all.

You can revert to the old behavior here, under "Counter mode".
